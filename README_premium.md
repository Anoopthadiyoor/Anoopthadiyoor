## About Me

I'm Anoop — an MCA student at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam, currently deep in the weeds of the MERN stack, and generally happiest when I'm turning a half-formed idea into something that runs.

My path into software started with the fundamentals — C, Python, Java — and grew into building real, end-to-end things: a machine learning model that predicts autism risk from a short questionnaire, and an augmented reality app that lets you drop furniture into your actual living room before you buy it. I like projects that touch a real problem, even a small one.

I'm not chasing buzzwords. I'm chasing the next thing I don't know how to build yet — and right now, that's full-stack JavaScript.
